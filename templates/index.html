The Rabbit Hole (The Rules)

(1) Humanity Forged Light for this Dark World

Illuminate Your Course. 


Shine at least Two nonorganic Light Sources while not at Rest.





(2) Man Is Whatever Room He Is In

Each Room is an opportunity for a New Story.


All Doors Close Immediately behind you.





(3) Focus = Mastery Of Life 1.0

No pausing. Of any kind (e.g. TV, books, songs). If you must re-consume something, you must restart it. 



(hints: One task at a time, two hands on task, operate at 0.9x your normal pace)


The Distraction Meter:

    • 0 to 1 for thoughts.

    • 1 Full Point for each Activity.

    • Half and quarter points for side activities (music, radio, background TV).


Keep your Distraction Meter low!





(4) What Is Your Control Level?

Control levels up with successful bets on acts that progress you toward your Objectives.


Make a bet on something you want out of life and assign it a value based on difficulty (say, between 0.25 and 4). Add that value to your Control Level when your Objective materializes.


If you Fail to materialize, you subtract the bet value multiplied by 0.5.
